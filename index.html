Roads
Roads is a traffic simulation game where your goal is to make as much money as possible. This is done in 2 ways. The first is by maximizing the amount of customers. This is done by providing a pathway from each house to every other house. But beware of traffic jams. Roads get slower as traffic increases. The second way to maximize profits is to deliver customers to their destination quickly. The payout per customer is determined by the time to distance ratio. In other words you can have 30 hours to go from coast to coast but only 5 to travel from state to state. All in all your task is to make the highest performance road system for the lowest cost.
